Chinese Pork Bone Stew

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs pork bones
  • 1 large carrot, sliced
  • 1 daikon radish, sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 piece of ginger, sliced
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice wine
  • Water
  • Optional: Chinese herbs (e.g., goji berries, red dates)

Instructions

  1. Blanch pork bones in boiling water for 5 minutes, then rinse.
  2. In a large pot, add pork bones, carrot, daikon, garlic, ginger, and enough water to cover.
  3. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer.
  4. Add soy sauce, rice wine, and salt to taste.
  5. Simmer for 2-3 hours until the broth is rich and flavorful.
  6. Optional: Add Chinese herbs in the last 30 minutes of cooking.
  7. Garnish with chopped green onions before serving.
