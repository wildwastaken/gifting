Perfect Steak

Ingredients

  • 1 ribeye steak
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, crushed
  • 1 sprig of rosemary
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Instructions

  1. Season the steak with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan over high heat.
  3. Add steak and sear for 3-4 minutes on each side.
  4. Add garlic, rosemary, and butter to the pan.
  5. Baste the steak with the melted butter for 1-2 minutes.
  6. Rest the steak for 5 minutes before serving.
